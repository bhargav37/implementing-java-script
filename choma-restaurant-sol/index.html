Welcome to Choma Restaurant

Choma Restauran is a one stop eating place where all of Nairobi's working population, local and foreign tourists visit to enjoy unique cuisine originally and carefully prepared by our leading chefs.

Our restaurant offers 24hour/7 days a week service to all our esteemed customers.

We also offer outside catering to event organizers. Our staff are dedicated to offering the best services to all our customers. We value our customers much and work to achieve the maximum possible customer satisfaction.

Try our service today and you'll get more than you bargained for.

Choma Restaurant's leading partners

Being the leading eating place in Kenya's capital, Nairobi, offering exceptional services, Choma restaurant has placed itself startegically at the disposal of giant companies and organizations in the capital.

2. Access Kenya
