Welcome to Choma restaurant Drinks menus

Choma Restaurant offers a dinner rich menu that ensures that our customers get what they are looking for. Below are select items on the menu that we are identify with.

1. The Gander Shiraz – Swartland.

Juicy, ripe fruit on the nose. Spice, chocolate and black fruit dominate with some wood spice. Smooth tannins and long, clean aftertaste. Gentle oak treatment ensure fruit forward wine with enough legs to mature a good 5 years

2. Moet & Chandon NV – Epernay.

Balanced and harmonious with mellow, well developed flavours. It expresses the rich diversity of the Champagne “terroir”.
